Watch through CCTV

CCTV cameras are an integral part of every classroom. We use these to make recordings of teachers teaching and class and help them improve the quality of teaching. These cameras will also provide streaming footage over the internet which will enable you to watch live classroom teaching from the comfort of your home. We feel cameras will enhance the learning environment in the classroom.

Technology in Education

Computers are being integrated into to day-to-day teaching from standard 1. Students are encouraged to learn and perform activities related to curriculum through programmes, games and activities designed specifically for learning. The students become computer-literate and at the same time improve their understanding of what is taught in the class. Computers are also used for supplementing class learning in various subjects.use of audio-visual aids shall be an integral part of the learning-teaching process which will aid in ensuring easier grasp of concepts.
